04 AI Lead Qualification Working

Real Estate — AI Lead
Qualifier & SMS Follow-Up

+

A buyer fills out one form — and that's the last time a human touches the lead. The submission fires a webhook into Make.com, where the Claude API scores the lead in 4 seconds against budget, pre-approval, and buying timeline, then writes the result straight back into the GoHighLevel CRM. From there, branching workflows take over: hot leads get an immediate call-me text, warm leads enter an SMS nurture sequence, cold leads get logged to a spreadsheet for the 30-day follow-up cycle. Every lead answered, scored, and routed — automatically.
